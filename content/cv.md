## Research Interests

My research interests include data fusion and data assimilation, high-dimensional statistics, and physics-informed statistical learning, motivated by applications in Earth and ocean sciences.

- **Data fusion and data assimilation:** statistically principled methods for integrating heterogeneous observations into dynamical Earth and ocean systems, with emphasis on scalable systems for real-world deployment.
- **Physics-informed statistical learning:** statistical models guided by physical knowledge and stochastic-process representations of uncertain physical processes.
- **High-dimensional statistics under computational constraints:** optimality of high-dimensional methods for large-scale data with latent structure.

## Projects and Interdisciplinary Experience

**Multi-sphere Interaction Data Integration in Western Pacific Ocean**, *2023 - Present*

- Developed a North Pacific ocean data assimilation and forecasting system using blockwise, multigrid, and spatially localized weighting strategies with physical constraints from Navier-Stokes equations.
- Achieved assimilation skill superior to a state-of-the-art benchmark from Mercator Ocean International.
- Established an end-to-end workflow covering observation quality control, assimilation, forecast, and evaluation on the Laoshan National Laboratory supercomputing platform.
- Developed real-time ocean current estimation and data-driven experimental design for adaptive control of autonomous observation platforms.

**High-resolution Ensemble Kalman Filter and Carbon Flux Fused Algorithm**, *2022 - Present*

- Developed a global carbon-flux data assimilation system.
- Proposed a multi-inflation strategy to jointly assimilate satellite and in situ CO2 observations, improving inversion stability and accuracy beyond benchmark products such as CarbonTracker.
- Coupled the system with GEOS-Chem for high-resolution global carbon-flux inversion and quantification of terrestrial and oceanic carbon sinks.
- Provided uncertainty diagnostics for carbon budget accounting and emission assessment; this work received a nomination for the Eni Award.
